About Us

At Financable, our mission is to empower small businesses by providing easy-to-use financial tools that simplify complex processes. We understand the challenges that entrepreneurs face, from managing payroll to navigating tax obligations. That's why we’ve developed solutions that are accessible, practical, and tailored to meet the unique needs of small business owners. Whether you're starting fresh or growing your business, our tools are designed to give you the financial clarity and control needed to thrive. We believe that when businesses have the right resources, they can focus more on what matters—building great products, supporting their customers, and making an impact in their communities. Let us take care of the financial details, so you can focus on growing your business with confidence.

Calculator

Our system makes it easy to calculate the total amount spent on employee salaries. All you need to do is enter the salaries for each employee, and we will automatically add them up for you. This gives you a quick and clear picture of your overall payroll costs, helping you manage your budget and financial planning. You can also adjust the calculation if needed, like focusing on specific teams or including things like bonuses or overtime pay.

Revenue

In this section our calculator makes it easy for you to track your finances by calculating your revenue based on employee expenses, business expenses, and income. Simply enter your employee costs (like salaries or benefits) and business expenses (such as rent, utilities, or supplies), along with your total income from sales or services. The calculator will do the math for you, subtracting your expenses from your income to show your net revenue. It’s a simple way to stay on top of your profits and make smarter financial decisions for your business.
